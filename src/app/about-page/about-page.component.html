About the Jokes
Once an experimental daily email sent out to a listserv of friends and family, Jokes On Me is now an experimental website open to ridicule and mocking from anyone courageous enough to stumble across the site. It was born from an interest in late night television monologues and jaw-aching respect for those that write them. Hopefully this site will bring you the daily chuckle, smile, or even milk spewing upset that every day deserves.
